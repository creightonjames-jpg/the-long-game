## Century Golf Partners culture-card material

This game's scoring model, mentor dialogue, and season narration draw
directly on the Century Golf Partners / Arnold Palmer Golf Management
culture card — including but not limited to:

- Quality Standards (Safety, Courtesy, Show, Efficiency)
- Steps of Service (Be Gracious, Be Great, Be Grateful)
- Daily Service Guidelines
- Service Recovery model (HEARD: Hear, Empathize, Apologize, Resolve, Diagnose)
- Team Values, Principles, Aspirations, Purpose, Mission, Vision, and Culture
  statements
